# 2026-07-08 本周 arXiv 前沿论文

### 1. From Fixed to Free Cameras: Calibration-Free View-Robust Vision-Language-Action Model
- **机构**：Wenhao Li, Xueying Jiang, Quanhao Qian, et al.
- **摘要**：解耦操控控制与相机几何，实现免校准的视域鲁棒性；仅需单目 RGB 图像与任务指令即可适应部署中相机的重新定位。
- **Link**：[arXiv:2607.05396](https://arxiv.org/abs/2607.05396)

### 2. Search Beyond What Can Be Taught: Evolving the Knowledge Boundary in Agentic Visual Generation
- **机构**：Haozhe Wang, Weijia Feng, Jinpeng Yu, et al.
- **摘要**：指出视觉生成器容易在训练数据之外虚构信息，提出结合搜索工具与协同训练框架以突破知识边界限制。
- **Link**：[arXiv:2607.05382](https://arxiv.org/abs/2607.05382)

### 3. What Does a Discrete Diffusion Model Learn?
- **机构**：Rodrigo Casado Noguerales, et al.
- **摘要**：推导 CTMC ELBO 并证明其唯一最优解为真实逆向跳跃率的条件期望，统一了 MDM 等多类损失函数。
- **Link**：[arXiv:2607.05381](https://arxiv.org/abs/2607.05381)

### 4. OptiAgent: End-to-End Optimization Modeling via Multi-Agent Iterative Refinement
- **机构**：Adriana Laurindo Monteiro, et al.
- **摘要**：提出多智能体框架，将运筹学问题描述自动转化为数学建模与代码，通过迭代修正在多项优化基准测试中表现优异。
- **Link**：[arXiv:2607.05346](https://arxiv.org/abs/2607.05346)

### 5. LLM-as-a-Verifier: A General-Purpose Verification Framework
- **机构**：Jacky Kwok, et al.
- **摘要**：提出通用验证框架，利用概率性评分生成连续分数以验证方案，无需训练即可提升智能体任务表现与强化学习样本效率。
- **Link**：[arXiv:2607.05391](https://arxiv.org/abs/2607.05391)

### 6. SovereignPA-Bench: Evaluating User-Owned Personal Agents under Evolving Intent, Platform Mediation, and Consent Constraints
- **机构**：Dylan Zongmin Liu, et al.
- **摘要**：提出个人代理基准，评估其在用户主权、隐私与同意约束下的性能；增强型主架构可显著减少隐私泄露及操控风险。
- **Link**：[arXiv:2607.05363](https://arxiv.org/abs/2607.05363)

### 7. Graph Sparse Sampling: Breaking the Curse of the Horizon in Continuous MDP Planning
- **机构**：Idan Lev-Yehudi, Vadim Indelman, et al.
- **摘要**：提出图稀疏采样算法，通过共享样本机制解决连续域不确定性规划难题，实现规划界限的多项式依赖。
- **Link**：[arXiv:2607.05359](https://arxiv.org/abs/2607.05359)

### 8. SteelBench: Evaluating Vision-Language Models in Real-World Industrial Environments
- **机构**：Suryanarayana Reddy Yarrabothula, et al.
- **摘要**：基于真实工厂监控视频评估视觉语言模型的活动识别与安全推理，结果显示模型表现显著差于人类且存在安全判断错误。
- **Link**：[arXiv:2607.05264](https://arxiv.org/abs/2607.05264)

### 9. Erasing Without Collateral Damage: Precise Concept Removal in Diffusion Models
- **机构**：Parth Upman, et al.
- **摘要**：基于子空间感知方向，无需参数调整即可精准删除扩散模型中的指定对象，有效减轻对相关概念的连带损伤。
- **Link**：[arXiv:2607.05274](https://arxiv.org/abs/2607.05274)

### 10. DSpark: Confidence-Scheduled Speculative Decoding with Semi-Autoregressive Generation
- **机构**：Xin Cheng, Xingkai Yu, Chenze Shao, et al.
- **摘要**：利用半自回归架构与置信调度验证优化大模型推理，减少服务器端验证浪费并提升生成速度。
- **Link**：[arXiv:2607.05147](https://arxiv.org/abs/2607.05147)
